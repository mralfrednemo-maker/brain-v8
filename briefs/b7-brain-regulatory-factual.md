# Regulatory Compliance Assessment: DORA Impact on Our Cloud Infrastructure

## Context

We are a mid-size fintech operating in the EU, providing payment processing services to 45 banks across 8 member states. Our infrastructure runs entirely on AWS eu-west-1 and eu-central-1 regions.

The Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) entered into application on January 17, 2025. Our compliance team believes we have until our next regulatory audit (scheduled Q4 2026) to demonstrate full compliance, but our legal counsel disagrees and says enforcement is already active.

## What we need to determine

1. Is DORA enforcement already active as of today, or is there a grace period for existing regulated entities?

2. What specific ICT risk management requirements under DORA apply to a payment processor of our size and profile?

3. Does our reliance on a single cloud provider (AWS) create a concentration risk finding under DORA Article 28-44 (ICT third-party risk)?

4. What are the actual penalties for non-compliance, and have any enforcement actions been taken against comparable entities since January 2025?

This is a factual assessment. We need to determine the ground truth on these regulatory questions before we can plan remediation. The models should converge on verifiable facts, not opinions.
