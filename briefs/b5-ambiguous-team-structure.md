# Team Structure Question

Our engineering organization is growing from 25 to 45 engineers over the next two quarters. Currently we have 4 feature teams of 5-7 people each, plus a 3-person platform team. The feature teams are organized by product area (Onboarding, Core Workflow, Integrations, Analytics).

Several problems have emerged:
- Cross-team dependencies are causing 2-3 week delays on features that span product areas
- The platform team is overwhelmed with requests from all four feature teams
- Two of the senior engineers want to move into management but we have no open management roles
- The Integrations team has the highest attrition (3 departures in 6 months) and lowest velocity

The VP of Engineering wants a proposal for how to restructure by Q3. There is no fixed set of options — we need to figure out what the right structure looks like given these constraints and the growth plan.

Budget for the reorg: we can create up to 3 new management positions and hire 2 additional senior engineers beyond the planned 20 new hires.
